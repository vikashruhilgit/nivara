## Task
**Goal:** Implement risk models: VaR (95%/99% historical simulation, 252d lookback, 30d minimum), Volatility (30d/90d annualized std dev of daily log returns), Drawdown from peak, Position risk score 0-100, and Correlation (Pearson on daily returns, 90d rolling), with proper handling for insufficient and missing data. Expose via GET /api/analysis/{symbol}/risk.

**Problem Statement:**
The platform needs quantitative risk assessment for individual instruments and portfolios. Without risk models, the Risk Meter (Job 16), recommendations (Job 17), and Risk Guardian (Job 20) cannot compute meaningful risk scores. Currently, no risk computation exists. Success looks like deterministic, reproducible risk metrics for any instrument with sufficient price history.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Given 252 days of AAPL returns, when VaR computed, then 95% and 99% percentiles returned (historical simulation)
- [ ] Given <30 days of data, when VaR requested, then returns "Insufficient data" status (VaR not computed)
- [ ] Given <30 days of data, when volatility requested, then computed but labeled "(estimated)"
- [ ] Given <30 days of data, when risk score requested, then uses sector-average proxy with "proxy-based" flag
- [ ] Given 90 days of daily returns for 2 instruments, when correlation computed, then Pearson coefficient returned
- [ ] Given portfolio of 5 stocks, when correlation matrix computed, then 5x5 Pearson matrix on 90d rolling window
- [ ] Given missing data <=5 days, when processing returns, then forward-fill applied
- [ ] Given missing data >5 days, then excluded from correlation with "insufficient" flag
- [ ] Given GET /api/analysis/{symbol}/risk, then returns VaR, volatility (30d/90d), drawdown, risk score, and data quality flags
- [ ] Given drawdown computation, when current price is 15% below peak, then drawdown = 15%
